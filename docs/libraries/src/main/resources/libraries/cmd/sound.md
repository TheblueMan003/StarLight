## play
- entity $selector
- mcobject $sound
- int $volume
- int $pitch
- mcobject $loc
Play the sound `sound` to `selector` with `volume` and `pitch` on channel `loc`
## play
- mcobject sound
- float volume
- float pitch

## play
- mcobject sound
- int pitch
Play the sound `sound` with volume = 1 and `pitch`
## play
